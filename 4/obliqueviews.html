There are two types of oblique projections
1. Cavalier
2. Cabinet

Cavalier Projection: This makes 45 degree angle with the projection plane. The projection of a line perpendicular to the view plane has the same length as the line itself in Cavalier projection. In a cavalier projection, the foreshortening factors for all three principal directions are equal.
